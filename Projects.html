3D Room Scanner

Using Lidar, a 360 degree camera and the HTC Vive controller, we are mapping the room to a 3D set of points,
and soon will be mapping textures from the camera to those points.

This model is created by waving the combined device throughout the room as though painting it, while keeping
track of the device's position using the Vive controller.


Leap Motion VR Interaction

The Leap is an accurate tool for tracking your hands and fingers in front of you.
Our current goal is to integrate the output of two of these sensors to gain an
even more accurate view of where your hands are, to improve accuracy even further
and allow for even more complex hand gestures.
